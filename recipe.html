Spider Cookies

Great for Kids of All Ages

These spider cookies take just moments to decorate, and they're a fun halloween treat for all the ghouls and goblins!

Ingredients:

  • orange icing
  • black piping
  • chocolate nonpareils
  • round sugar cookies

Instructions:

  1. Ice each of the sugar cookies with a full layer of the orange icing.
  2. Criscross four lines of black piping on the cookie so they all intersect at the center, creating 8 legs.
  3. Press a chocolate nonpareil into the center of the cookie, over the black piping "legs" and into the orange icing. This is the spider's body.
  4. And there you have it! Enjoy!
